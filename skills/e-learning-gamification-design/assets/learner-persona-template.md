## Example Persona

### Sarah - The Career Climber

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Name** | Sarah Chen |
| **Role/Title** | Marketing Specialist |
| **Age Range** | 28-35 |
| **Experience Level** | Intermediate |
| **Organization Type** | Corporate (Mid-size) |

**Background**: MBA graduate with 5 years of experience. Comfortable with technology. Learns during lunch breaks and commutes.

**Goals**: Advance to management role within 2 years. Develop leadership and data analysis skills.

**Challenges**: Limited time (works 50+ hours/week). Needs practical, immediately applicable content.

**Preferences**: Video content (watches on mobile during commute). Self-paced. Likes clear progress indicators.

**Player Type**: Achiever - motivated by badges, certificates, and visible progress.

**Quote**: "I need learning that fits into my schedule and gives me skills I can use tomorrow."
